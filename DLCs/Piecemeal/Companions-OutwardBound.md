# Piecemeal

## Companion: Doctor Onward
- Faction: Outward Bound
- Class: Magician
- Races: Dictum+, Neutral
- Cost: 15
- Power: 1
- Teamwork: 5
- Sociability: 6
- Morality: 6
- Positive: + Pampers his favorites
- Positive: + Excellent dungeon master
- Negative: - Contagious mood swings
- Negative: - Irritable and harsh
- Mixed: +/- Perpetually discontent and perfectionist
- Mixed: +/- Enjoys playing cult leader and has a harem
- Has: Onward has Portals 5, Aethernautics 5, Domain 4, Demiplanes 6, *Oracle, Keeper(Implement, Demense, Eromancy), & Technopath*
- Quote: _*“We're all here because of him, really. Mentally as much as physically. <u>Of course</u> it goes to his head.” - Mira Startome.*_

Born in Victorian London at the height of industrialization, Jasper Onward seems to think progress any slower than that is unacceptable, even if you're immortal. He offends as many movers and shakers as he impresses, and flaunts his pride of *both*. His standards for everyone are dauntingly high, but he seems to have a gift for pulling people up to meet them. Pulling him out of funks when *he* falls short of them is usually a three-woman job.


## Companion: Stella Summerborn
- Faction: Outward Bound
- Class: Sorceress
- Race: Daeva
- Cost: 11
- Power: 6
- Teamwork: 6
- Sociability: 5
- Morality: 9
- Positive: + Spectacular physical fighter
- Positive: + Never forgets a friend
- Negative: - Not over her dead ex
- Negative: - Keeps herself very busy
- Mixed: +/- Will throw herself into a relationship *hard* when she's up to trying one
- Mixed: +/- She who fights monsters gets really hecking good at it, but there may be side effects.
- Has: Stella has Portals 5, Windkeeping 5, Metallurgy 4, Demiplanes 3, Swordsinging 5, *Charge Swap(Sword), Memorize, Gunwitch, Soul Warrior(Rapier)*
- Quote: _*“Absolutely no one I'd rather have watching my back. I just hope she remembers how to relax eventually.” - Doctor Onward.*_

Born in the Summer Court from Stackhold emigrants, Stella's disgust at her girlfriend's true death sent her looking for the opposite extreme. Now she's head of security for the Outward Bound, which means she's fought more minor Outsiders personally than just about anyone. She's probably only here for a century, but she'll leave with more favors, and friends, than she's noticed.


## Companion: Ellie Feliciano
- Faction: Outward Bound
- Class: Warlock
- Race: Neutral
- Cost: 4
- Power: 4
- Teamwork: 8
- Sociability: 9
- Morality: 6
- Positive: + Friends across fifty worlds
- Positive: + Can cook and mix anything she's seen twice
- Positive: + Organizes the best parties
- Negative: - Thinks she's the least-cool friend
- Negative: - Never believes any of her skills are hard
- Negative: - Always busy with the next party
- Mixed: +/- Looking to move on past *just* being a party girl but doesn't know what to do next
- Has: Ellie has Alchemy 3, Portals 2, Hexes 2, Waterworking 2, *Fascinate, Maid Hand, & Poisoner (Booze counts.)*
- Quote: _*“Best parties, cocktails, and cooking in town that isn't invite-only. And if the Doc isn't on the invite list, better than those too.” - James Wagner.*_

Ellie has absolutely no head for research and says she's 'barely a witch'; she rarely understands any of the theory. But none of the Bounders care, because she's excited about the practical results, learns entire cuisines as easily as bad cooks learn recipes, and is fantastic at organizing parties and big events. She followed a crush to Outward Bound and fell in love with the place; there's better partiers other places but the intense appreciation from unwinding researchers is intoxicating.


## Companion: James Wagner
- Faction: Outward Bound
- Class: Magician
- Race: Elf
- Cost: 8
- Power: 6
- Teamwork: 5
- Sociability: 3
- Morality: 4
- Positive: + Great at explaining and teaching
- Positive: + Shares his stash freely
- Positive: + Endless energy for a project
- Negative: - If he leaves his research to *try*
- Negative: - Whether you asked for it or not
- Negative: - Abrasive and misanthropic
- Mixed: +/- Sees a vision for his friend's potential and makes them his project. Doesn't ask.
- Has: James has Runes 4, Aethernautics 4, Domain 4, Demiplanes 4, Portals 3, Naturalism 4, *Suggestion, Keeper(Omnimancy: Theory, Resurrection, Dominate, Teleport)*
- Quote: _*“There's a lot of people in the Bound pretty obsessive, and pretty talented - but he might be the one who's most both.” - Lulissa Match, Bounder researcher.*_

Many people move to the Bound to get away from their past, in whatever community they came from. Not nearly as many moved to get away from society in general. A Hawthorne graduate, James stayed there for several years, but found it was still full of people he couldn't stand. A chance meeting in a session where the Doctor gave his pitch, and he jumped ship immediately, as taken by the research as by being an awkward portal trip away from society. He's in artist and alchemist circles, experimenting with how Aethernautics interacts with drugs and aesthetics, but mostly he's a 70 hours a week researcher.
